Cookie Policy

Cookie Policy for GameArea

This is the Cookie Policy for GameArea, accessible via https://gamearea.site.

What Are Cookies

This site, like almost all professional websites, uses cookies to improve your experience. Cookies are small files that a website downloads to your device. This page explains the information they collect, how we use it, and why we sometimes store these cookies. Disabling cookies may break some functionality.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several reasons. Unfortunately, there are no universal options for disabling cookies without also disabling features. It's recommended to keep cookies enabled if you're unsure.

Disabling Cookies

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that doing so may affect this and many other websites. For the best experience, we recommend keeping cookies enabled.

The Cookies We Set

  • Account-related cookies: Help manage signups and settings; may persist after logout.
  • Login-related cookies: Keep you signed in as you navigate pages; deleted upon logout.
  • Email/newsletter cookies: Store preferences for newsletter popups and notifications.
  • Order processing cookies: Required for processing purchases and carts correctly.
  • Survey cookies: Track who completed surveys or deliver accurate results.
  • Forms cookies: Remember form input for future convenience (like contact forms).
  • Site preference cookies: Store preferences for layout, dark mode, language, etc.

Third Party Cookies

In some cases, we use cookies from trusted third parties. Below are details:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks site usage to help us improve experience and content. Learn more on the Google Analytics Cookie Guide.
  • Google AdSense: Delivers more relevant ads and limits repeated ads using a DoubleClick cookie. See the Google AdSense FAQ.
  • A/B Testing: Cookies are used during tests to ensure a consistent and optimized experience.
  • Purchase tracking: Helps us calculate conversion rates for business insights.
  • Affiliate tracking: Tracks visits from partner sites for commissions and bonuses.
  • Social media: Integrations like Facebook, Instagram, etc., may set cookies to enhance your profile and content interaction.

More Information

We hope this clarifies our use of cookies. If unsure whether you need cookies, it's safer to keep them enabled in case they interact with features you use.
